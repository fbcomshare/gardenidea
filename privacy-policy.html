Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

GardenBloom ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit GardenBloom (gardenbloomblog.com).

Information We Collect

  • Personal Data: Name and email address when you subscribe to our newsletter or contact us.
  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on site — collected automatically.
  • Cookies: We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve your experience.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our website
  • To send newsletters (only if you subscribe)
  • To display personalised advertisements via third-party ad networks
  • To analyse website traffic and improve user experience
  • To process digital guide purchases via Gumroad
  • To comply with legal obligations

Display Advertising — Google AdSense & Ezoic

We use Ezoic (powered by Google AdSense) to display advertisements. These third-party vendors may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites. You may opt out at Google Ads Settings.

Ezoic uses cookies and similar technologies to optimise ad performance. For more information, visit Ezoic's Privacy Policy.

Digital Guide Purchases

All digital guide purchases are processed securely through Gumroad. GardenBloom does not store your payment information. Please review Gumroad's Privacy Policy for information on how your purchase data is handled.

Affiliate Disclosure

GardenBloom participates in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program and other affiliate programs. When you click affiliate links and make purchases, we may receive a small commission at no additional cost to you. See our full Disclaimer for details.

Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your experience. You may instruct your browser to refuse cookies or to indicate when a cookie is sent. Note that disabling cookies may affect some website functionality.

Types of cookies we use: Essential (site function), Analytics (Google Analytics), Advertising (Ezoic/AdSense).

Third-Party Services

  • Google Analytics — traffic analysis
  • Ezoic — ad management and optimisation
  • Gumroad — digital product sales
  • Formspree — contact form processing

GDPR Rights (European Users)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the right to access, update, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us via our contact page.

CCPA (California Users)

California residents have the right to know what personal information we collect, the right to delete it, and the right to opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information.

Children's Privacy

GardenBloom is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

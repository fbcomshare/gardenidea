About Gardenidea

Helping you create beautiful gardens, floral crafts, and homes — one idea at a time.

Our Story

Gardenidea was built by a team of garden enthusiasts and floral craft lovers who believe that beautiful spaces shouldn't require a professional budget or years of experience. Whether you have a sprawling backyard or a tiny balcony, a love of ribbon crafts or a passion for cottage gardens, we're here to give you the ideas, information, and guides to make it happen.

We publish deeply informative content on lawn care, garden design, floral crafts, home decor, outdoor living, string lights, and seasonal inspiration. Our digital guides are designed for complete beginners — clear, illustrated, and actionable from day one.

What We Cover

Lawn & garden ideas, cottagecore and cottage garden design, ribbon rose crafts, DIY wedding bouquets, seasonal wreaths, farmhouse home decor, outdoor living spaces, string light ideas, backyard transformations, and much more.
